# FUTURE_DS_02
Customer Retention &amp; Churn Analysis (Excel Dashboard) 
📌 Project Overview  This project focuses on analyzing customer churn and retention patterns using Excel. The goal was to identify key drivers of customer churn and uncover insights that can help improve customer retention and business performance.

Objectives
Analyze customer churn behavior
Identify high-risk customer segments
Understand the relationship between tenure, contracts, and churn
Explore how retention impacts revenue
📂 Dataset

The dataset includes:

Customer demographics (e.g., gender)
Contract types (Month-to-month, One year, Two year)
Tenure (customer lifespan)
Monthly and total charges
Churn status (Yes/No)

🛠️ Tools & Techniques
Microsoft Excel
PivotTables
Pivot Charts
Slicers (for interactivity)
Calculated metrics (Churn Rate, Retention Rate)

📊 Dashboard Features
KPI cards:
Churn Rate
Retention Rate
Total Customers
Average Monthly Revenue
Visualizations:
Churn vs Retention (Donut Chart)
Churn by Tenure
Churn by Contract Type
Churn by Gender
Revenue vs Tenure

Interactive Filters:
Churn
Contract Type
Tenure
Internet Service

🔍 Key Insights
Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn
New customers (0–12 months) are the most likely to churn
Churn decreases as customer tenure increases
Long-term customers contribute more to total revenue

💡 Business Recommendations
Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts
Improve onboarding experience for new customers
Implement retention strategies targeting early-stage customers
Focus on customer loyalty programs to increase lifetime value
